# Sales Performance Analysis

## Business Problem
The company wants to understand which products and regions generate the most revenue.

## Tools Used
- Excel
- Data Cleaning
- Pivot Tables

## Analysis Performed
- Revenue by region
- Revenue by product
- Monthly sales trends

## Key Insights
- Region A generates highest revenue
- Product X has declining sales
- Q4 has peak sales

## Business Recommendations
- Increase marketing in high-performing regions
- Re-evaluate Product X pricing strategy
